## 📖 About the Project

Planet Protectors is an educational game designed to teach primary school children (ages 5–12) about environmental sustainability through fun and interactive gameplay.

Players complete a series of educational challenges focused on important environmental topics such as recycling, pollution, renewable energy, ocean conservation and waste management. Instead of simply reading information, children actively learn by answering questions, solving challenges and progressing through different levels.

Originally developed as my A-Level Computer Science coursework, Planet Protectors is now being redeveloped as a long-term software engineering portfolio project while I complete my Level 4 Software Development Apprenticeship.

The aim is to create a game that is both educational and engaging, making it suitable for use in classrooms as well as at home.

---

## 🎯 Why I Built Planet Protectors

Research carried out during the planning stage identified a gap in the market for educational games that specifically focus on environmental awareness for younger children.

Planet Protectors aims to:

- 🌱 Teach children about protecting the environment.
- ♻️ Encourage sustainable habits from an early age.
- 🎮 Make learning interactive and enjoyable.
- 🏫 Support both classroom and home learning.
- 🌍 Raise awareness of real-world environmental issues.
